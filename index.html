About This Site

Created by: Louisa Molin

Can You Go Net-Zero calculates the amount of trees you would need to plant to go "net-zero" based on your carbon footprint. Click "Get Started" to answer a few questions and see if you can go net-zero today.

Disclaimer

The carbon footprint calculated by this site is highly inaccurate and should be used for demonstrational purposes only. To find your real carbon footprint, check out the EPA link on the right.
