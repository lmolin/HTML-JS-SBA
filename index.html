About This Site

Created by: Louisa Molin

Can You Go Net-Zero calculates the amount of trees you would need to plant to go "net-zero" based on your carbon footprint. Click "Get Started" to answer a few questions and see if you can go net-zero today.

About Net-Zero

Net-zero refers to carbon neutrality, the state of having all carbon emissions reabsorbed by carbon sinks such as forests. Because of this, carbon neutrality does not necessitate truly zero emissions, but merely net-zero emissions.
